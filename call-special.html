  • Special Session Title :

  • Aims and Objective:

    The special session, “control and protection of distribution system with high penetration of renewable energy” try to find to endorse and disseminate knowledge on the various issues and technologies of renewable energy based generation and distribution systems. The control and protection of distributed energy resources (DERs) based distribution system is highly affected due to the bi-directional power flow and diverse operating mode. Therefore, effective control and protection of DERs based distribution network is a challenging task for the utility system. The special session aims to serve researchers from academia and industry, engineers, economists, manufacturers, NGOs, and societies to help them keep well-informed of new advances in their expertize fields and to apply unconventional energy based solutions to present practices.

  • Topics of Interest :

    Following are the related topics for this special session, but not limited to:

    • Biomass conversion, control and protection
    • Photovoltaic technology conversion, control and protection
    • Solar thermal applications
    • Wind energy technology: conversion, control and protection
    • Solar and low energy architecture
    • Climatology and meteorology
    • Geothermal technology: conversion, control and protection
    • Wave, tide and ocean thermal energies: conversion, control and protection
    • Hydro power: conversion, control and protection
    • Hydrogen production technology and fuel cells
    • AC,DC and Hybrid Microgrid control and protection
